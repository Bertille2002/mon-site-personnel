Raising Awareness

Since 1986, the primary mission of Save Mother Earth has been to educate the public on the dangers of human behavior in this post industrial world. This foundation has provided factual educational information to the public about environmental awareness.

Projects and Initiatives

By supporting our Global Initiatives, the non-profit public benefit company Save the Earth Foundation works to contribute to the improvements required to ensure a healthier future for our planet. Our mission is to provide funding for the single most significant environmental changes occurring globally in an attempt to halt and eventually reverse the planet Earth's degradation.
